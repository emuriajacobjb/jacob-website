welcome to community water and sanitation intiatives

Our mission is to ensure every household in our community has access to clean,safe water and proper sanitation facilities

Problem identified

  • Water pollution problems in the community Poor sanitation leading to diseases

    • Many families in our community struggle with unsafe drinking water and inadequate sanitation facilities,leading to frequent outbreaks of water borne diseases

    Objectives of the intiatives.

    • To provide clean and safe water to every household To improve sanitation and hygiene practices To educate communities about water conservation To promote eco friendly waste management

    • Activities

    • Installing boreholes or water tanks Organizing hygiene education programmes Building latrines

    • Expected outcomes

    • Reduction in waterborne diseases Sustainable water uses Improved health and wellbeing

    • Join us in making clean water and sanitation a reality for everyone
